Mary Soroka is an award-winning Art, Film and Creative Director. Prior to her current role as Global Associate Creative Director at Edelman, the world's largest independent PR firm, Mary climbed the ranks over 8 years from Jr. Art Director to Associate Creative Director at Leo Burnett Canada.

2

Known for her visual acuity and ability to shapeshift aesthetically into any style or subject matter, Mary has created internationally awarded advertising campaigns for a number of world-class clients, including IKEA and TD Bank. In 2021, she served on the jury for the National Advertising Challenge.
