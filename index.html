About

Mary Soroka is an award-winning art, film, and creative director on a mission to make everything look and feel amazing. Prior to taking on her current role as Global Associate Creative Director at the world's largest independent PR firm, Mary climbed the ranks over the course of 8 years from Junior Art Director to Associate Creative Director at Leo Burnett Toronto.

Experience + Recognition

A lifelong art/design obsessive, Mary is known for her visual acuity and ability to aesthetically shapeshift among a huge variety of brands, styles, and subject matter. She has won a number of local and international awards, and developed creative campaigns for a number of world-class clients, with notable examples being IKEA, Samsung, Bell, TD Bank, and Dove.
