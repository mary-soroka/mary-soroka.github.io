November 2018

New Orleans, USA

In 1915, healer and rumoured voodoo priestess Julia Brown prophesies that she will take her entire town of Frenier with her when she dies. No one listens. Years later, upon Julia's passing, everyone in town attends her funeral. The same day, the New Orleans Hurricane of 1915 strikes. The Category 4 storm sweeps through Louisiana, flattening small towns and killing all of the Frenier townsfolk. Locals believe Julia still haunts their swampland graveyard in LaPlace, Louisiana.
