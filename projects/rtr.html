Raising The Roof
To raise awareness of Raising The Roof's youth prevention programs, we offered a glimpse into the not-so-joyful side that some experience during the holidays. Holiday cards were sent to donors across Canada. On the outside, cards shared a peaceful message, but when opened, the sentiment would change, and in place of typical holiday music, chilling audio from a conflicted home.
To tell our story in radio, we juxtaposed the sounds of conflict in the home against the quieter sounds of the street, forcing listeners to consider the grim dilemma facing youth at risk of homelessness.
