Raising the Roof

To raise awareness of RTR's youth prevention programs, we offered a glimpse into the not-so-joyful side that some experience during the holidays. Holiday cards were sent to donors across Canada. On the outside, cards shared a peaceful message, but when opened, the sentiment would change, and in place of typical holiday music, chilling audio from a conflicted home.
Art Director: Mary Soroka
Copywriter: Shauna Roe
Producer: Alexandra Postans
