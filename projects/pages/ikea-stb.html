IKEA Share The Bathroom
To help people tell others how sharing a bathroom could be better, we gave Canadians the power to create custom IKEA bathroom ads that were served up on their shared network. That way their family members, roommates, co-workers or significant others would see them when they were online, which was a media first.

The results? 800,000+ custom ads were served. IKEA saw a 12% increase in bathroom sales, with a whopping 34% increase in online sales.
