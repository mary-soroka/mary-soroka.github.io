Knixteen

Knixteen 'Oh-No' Proof Underwear are a teen girl's best defence against life-ruining period leaks. To launch the product, we tapped into the insight that all women and girls know too well: that our period always seems to arrive at the worst possible moments. We created a series of online videos, featuring different period personalities, and situations where girls may fear their period most. Our message was simple: Hey period, STFU.
