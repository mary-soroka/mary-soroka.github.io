Dodgeball Canada
The sport of dodgeball was in need of a serious facelift. No one took it seriously - ranking it behind race walking and just ahead of darts. With the World Dodgeball Championships coming to Toronto in 2017, it was the perfect opportunity to change perceptions and gain legitimacy, no wrenches needed.
