AdAge
Cover Design
Contest

For their annual cover design competition, AdAge asked entrants to address the importance of truthful representations in media.

We believe the figures who inspire all of us should be representative of all of us, so our entry posed the question: isn't it time we rethink who fits the mold of today's greatest role models?
