Street-Level Activation

IKEA
Making Our
Own Power

IKEA is the largest wind-farm operator in Canada, and generates three times more energy than it needs for all of its Canadian stores. So to highlight IKEA's energy independence, on Earth Day, in the middle of Canada's version of energy-hungry Times Square in Toronto, we created a sign that was 100% powered by people. And rewarded participants with power saving LED light bulbs.
