Saulxures-sur-Moselotte, France

In Saulxures we ate tartiflettes and drank Vin d'Alsace and met newborn calves and stargazed off the mountains. I also stargazed at posters of my boyfriend's uncle, winner of the 16th stage of the 1997 Tour de France. (Everyone in the family has a beautiful pair of legs.)

Paris, France

The first time I visited Paris, I hated it. Returning on the arm of a French man made all the difference. Now we reminisce about wandering up to the Sacré-Cœur with a bottle of wine at night before taking the train to his beautiful mountain hometown in the northeast.
